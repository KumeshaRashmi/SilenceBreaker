# Finding Support Organisations (General Information)

Source note: General signposting information. Replace the placeholders below with verified, up-to-date services for your country and CITE them in your report.

Many countries have free, confidential services for people affected by domestic abuse or harassment, including:
- National helplines (often available by phone, and sometimes by text or online chat).
- Local support centres and advocacy services.
- Services that help with safety planning, housing, and accessing other support.
- Specialist services for specific communities or needs.

When contacting a service, you do not usually have to decide anything straight away. Trained workers can listen, explain options, and help you think through next steps at your own pace.

If you are searching online, consider using a safer device if you think yours may be monitored, and be aware of browser history.
